## QUARTER 1: Logic, Proofs, and Foundations (Lessons 1-22)

### Unit 1: Conditional Statements (Lessons 1-3)
- **Lesson 1.1**: Introduction to Conditional Statements
  - Define "if-then" statements
  - Distinguish hypothesis from conclusion
  - Apply conditional reasoning to geometry
- **Lesson 1.2**: Converse, Inverse, and Contrapositive
  - Write converse, inverse, and contrapositive
  - Determine truth values of related statements
- **Lesson 1.3**: Practice with Conditional Statements
  - Logical equivalence (statement and contrapositive)
  - Biconditional statements ("if and only if")
  - Interpret "all" and "not" statements

## QUARTER 2: Triangles, Congruence, and Coordinate Geometry (Lessons 23-43)

### Unit 11: Triangle Theorems (Lessons 23-25)
- **Lesson 11.1**: Triangle Angle Relationships

  - Exterior angles sum to 360
- **Lesson 11.2**: Triangle Inequality and Isosceles Triangles
  - Triangle inequality theorem
  - Base angles of isosceles triangles are congruent
- **Lesson 11.3**: Medians, Midsegments, and Proofs

  - Midsegment is parallel to third side and half its length
